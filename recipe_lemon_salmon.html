Lemon Herb Salmon

U
Prep time: 25 min • Category: Dinner
Tags: healthy, quick

Description

Flaky salmon with lemon, herbs, and garlic, roasted to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets
  • Lemon
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh dill
  • Salt, pepper

How to make

  1. Preheat oven and line a baking tray.
  2. Season salmon with lemon, garlic, oil, and dill.
  3. Bake until flaky and serve.
