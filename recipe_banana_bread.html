Cinnamon Banana Bread

U
Prep time: 15 min • Category: Baking
Tags: sweet, easy

Description

Moist banana bread with a warm cinnamon flavor, perfect for a cozy snack.

Ingredients

  • Ripe bananas
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon

How to make

  1. Mix wet ingredients and mashed bananas.
  2. Fold in dry ingredients and cinnamon.
  3. Bake until golden and a skewer comes out clean.
