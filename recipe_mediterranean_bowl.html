Mediterranean Bowl

U
Prep time: 10 min • Category: Lunch
Tags: fresh, vegetarian

Description

Colorful bowl with chickpeas, crunchy veggies, and a lemon dressing.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Cucumber
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Feta
  • Olives
  • Lemon

How to make

  1. Chop vegetables and combine with chickpeas.
  2. Whisk lemon and olive oil for dressing.
  3. Toss, add feta and olives, and serve.
