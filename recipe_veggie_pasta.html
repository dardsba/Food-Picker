Quick Veggie Pasta

U
Prep time: 20 min • Category: Dinner
Tags: vegan, fast, easy

Description

Simple, fresh pasta with tomatoes, garlic, and basil for a quick weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil
  • Salt, pepper

How to make

  1. Cook pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Saute garlic in olive oil, add tomatoes and soften.
  3. Toss pasta with sauce, add basil, season to taste.
